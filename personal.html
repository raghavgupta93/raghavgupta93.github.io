Languages

Foreign languages hold the same allure to me as mysteries do to detectives. More than getting a door into a new culture, I am fascinated by the idea of speaking in a self-sufficient way of expression that somehow few around me can understand.

My language endeavours have led me to formally study Spanish and Russian and Swahili, and most times when I am not working, I am beefing up my scores on Duolingo, which has to be my favorite website.

As an aside, if you can read IPA, my name's pronunciation is very close to /ˈraːghəv ˈgʊptaː/.

What's new here:
